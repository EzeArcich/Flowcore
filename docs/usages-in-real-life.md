# Usage In Real Life

## Scenario: finding a company
You find a company on LinkedIn or Google.

### Step 1
Create the company.

### Step 2
Add every useful contact:
- recruiter
- founder
- CTO
- general contact email
- LinkedIn profiles

### Step 3
Send the first outreach and register it as an interaction.

### Step 4
Set a follow-up date.

### Step 5
Every day, open the dashboard and act on:
- overdue follow-ups
- due today follow-ups
- sent quotations pending reply

### Step 6
When a company replies, register the inbound interaction.

### Step 7
If they ask for pricing, create a quotation.

### Step 8
Advance the company through the pipeline until won or lost.
